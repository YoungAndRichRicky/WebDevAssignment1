Donations We offer


- Monetary Donations -

Direct financial contributions can be made by individuals to initiatives and organizations devoted to sea turtle conservation. These funds are used to support a variety of initiatives, including community engagement, habitat protection, animal rescue and rehabilitation, and research.

- Equipment and Resource Donations -

Donors can contribute the specialized tools or materials required for efforts to conserve sea turtles. This can include equipment for monitoring and researching sea turtles, such as boats, satellite tags, GPS units, nest markers, or research instruments.

- Symbolic Adoptions -

Adopting a sea turtle symbolically is possible through donation-based adoption schemes. Donors receive an adoption certificate and updates on the progress of their adopted turtle in exchange for their donation. The money is used towards sea turtle care and conservation initiatives.

- In-Kind Donations -

Donations of goods or services that directly aid sea turtle conservation efforts might be made in place of monetary contributions by private persons or companies. This could involve giving out office supplies, building supplies, technology, or expert services like graphic design or legal advice.
