Bentota Turtle Hatchery


In Sri Lanka, the Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a sanctuary dedicated to the protection and conservation of sea turtles. The hatchery, which is located in the picturesque seaside community of Bentota, offers a secure environment for nesting, hatching, and rehabilitation for these magnificent animals.

Turtles Species in Kosgoda

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery understands how crucial it is to protect vulnerable habitats and endangered sea turtle species. Their committed team of professionals and volunteers put in many hours to safeguard the survival and well-being of these amazing aquatic creatures. The Green Turtle , Olive Ridley Turtle , Loggerhead Turtle , Hawksbill Turtle , and Leatherback Turtle are just a few of the turtle species that call the hatchery home. Every species has distinctive traits and adds to the diverse biodiversity of our coastal habitats.

Conservation Efforts

A variety of conservation initiatives are currently being carried out at the Bentota Turtle Hatchery to safeguard and preserve sea turtles. To safeguard the survival and well-being of these wonderful creatures, their committed team and volunteers put in endless effort. The following are a few of the major conservation projects the Bentota Turtle Hatchery has undertaken:

Egg Collection and Incubation

When sea turtle eggs are laid on exposed beaches, they carefully gather them and transport them to their hatchery. With the eggs safe from predators and in a regulated environment, they can ensure a higher rate of successful hatching.


Hatchling Release

The crew conducts carefully planned releases of sea turtle hatchlings into the water after they have emerged from their eggs. Their team makes sure the hatchlings have the best chance of making it to the ocean safely because this stage is crucial to their survival.


Public Awareness and Education

They run public education campaigns and educational activities to raise awareness of the value of protecting sea turtles. We aim to increase awareness and motivate conservation action through narrated tours, educational exhibits, and engaging activities.


Nesting Site Protection

To prevent any disturbance, they locate and protect breeding locations along the coast. Their team keeps a careful eye on these locations to guard them against risks like human encroachment and predation.


Advocacy and Policy Support

At the local and federal levels, they push for the adoption of stricter conservation laws and policies. In order to guarantee the protection and preservation of sea turtles and their ecosystems, they also work in conjunction with governmental and non-governmental groups.

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery contributes significantly to the protection of sea turtles and their environments through these extensive conservation initiatives. You can aid with the preservation of these gorgeous creatures and help secure their existence for future generations by funding their hatchery.
